STAND UP FOR CHANGE TODAY

The Federation of Engineers, Technicians and Technologists of Uganda(FETU) is an organization dedicated to championing the interests of Engineers, Technologists, Technicians & Artisans in Uganda. Our mission is to advocate for better salaries, minimum wage standards, improved job opportunities, enhanced working conditions & ultimately the betterment of every individual in our field.

HOW WE HELP

Advocate for better salaries


Professionals in Uganda should be paid the same as their counterparts in other countries. FETU aims to rectify that.




Enhanced Work Conditions


Professionals deserve good work environments. We are against all kinds of workplace injustice including toxic environments, discrimination, segregation etc..



Education


We provide education and training facilities to our Engineers, Technologists, Technicians & Artisans in Uganda
